Welcome to NFTMarket: Click to buy an NFT with LN Bitcoin

1- Download Hiro wallet extension for your browser

2- Click below to start swapping LN Bitcoin for STX

Integration Instructions for Stacks App Developers

Merchant (NFT Market) would integrate the swap tool as below:


        <div id="root"></div>
        <script src="https://lnswap-widget.vercel.app/widget.js"  
                id="LNSwap-Widget-Script" 
                data-config="{'name': 'lnswap', 'config': {'targetElementId': 'root'}}" >
        </script>

Merchant (NFT Market) would then initiate the swap in their frontend as below: 


            lnswap('swap', 'user stx address', 'amount in STX');

        e.g. lnswap('swap', 'SP1GH2VFAZ7ZB02JHBTMJSQYKJ83ESCNBZ0S3198R', '50');
